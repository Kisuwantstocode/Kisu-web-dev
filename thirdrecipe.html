Ingredients

  1. Tea leaves (Chai Patti)
  2. Sugar
  3. water
  4. Milk

Instructions

  1. Boil water in a pan
  2. Add tea leaves in the pan
  3. boil for 1-2 minutes
  4. Add sugar
  5. Add milk
  6. boil for 1 more minute
  7. Strain into a Cup after sieving
  8. Enjoy!
